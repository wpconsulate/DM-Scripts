DataTables Editor - styled for stacked inputs

When creating a web-site that utilises Editor, you will very likely want to style the Editor components to fit in with the rest of your web-site. Editor is designed to be completely customisable with CSS in terms of styling and through plug-ins in terms of the displayed presentation components.

This example shows how a trivial CSS change change effect the layout of the form, in this case stacking the form elements horizontally rather than in the two column layout found in most of the other examples.
